Privacy & Data Protection Policy

CodeLingo Official Documentation

Effective Date: April 5, 2026 | Version 1.1

At CodeLingo, we believe that learning to code should not come at the cost of your privacy. We are committed to protecting the personal data of our users through transparent data practices and industry-standard security protocols.

Core Principle: We only collect data that is strictly necessary to provide a gamified learning experience. We never sell your data to third-party brokers.

1. Data Collection & Source

We process information provided directly by you and generated through your interaction with the App:

  • Authentication Data: When you use Google Sign-In, we receive your unique User ID, email, and profile name. This is handled securely via Firebase Authentication.
  • Educational Progress: To provide a continuous experience, we store your XP (Experience Points), completed modules, daily streaks, and "Heart" balances.
  • Technical Identifiers: We collect anonymized device IDs and crash reports to maintain app stability and optimize performance across different Android versions.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is processed under the following legal bases:

  • Service Delivery: Managing your account and synchronizing your progress across devices.
  • Gamification: Calculating leaderboard positions and streak rewards.
  • Communications: Sending critical system updates or "Daily Reminder" notifications to help you stay on track with your coding goals.

3. Third-Party Integrations & Security

CodeLingo integrates with top-tier service providers who comply with international privacy standards:

  • Google Firebase: Secure cloud infrastructure for data storage and real-time syncing.
  • AdMob by Google: Used for serving rewarded ads. We do not share your personal learning history with advertisers.
  • RevenueCat: A secure bridge for managing Apple App Store and Google Play subscriptions without storing your credit card details on our servers.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your information as long as your account is active. Users have the Right to be Forgotten. You can request the full deletion of your account and associated data at any time through the app settings or by contacting us via email.

5. Global Compliance (GDPR, KVKK, CCPA)

Regardless of your location, we aim to provide a high standard of privacy rights, including the right to access your data, the right to rectification, and the right to data portability.

6. Security Measures

We implement End-to-End Encryption (E2EE) for sensitive data transfers and utilize Firebase’s robust security rules to ensure that only you can access your personal learning data.
